About Jubex Properties

Building trust through verified properties across Nigeria

Who We Are

Jubex Properties is a trusted real estate company based in Nigeria, committed to helping individuals and businesses find safe, secure, and valuable properties to invest in. With a focus on land, houses, and rental units, we simplify the process of buying and selling properties through transparency, integrity, and local knowledge.

Our Mission

To connect people to genuine properties that improve their lives and investments, while offering honest guidance and unmatched customer support.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria’s most reliable real estate brand by delivering verified property listings, ethical services, and long-term client satisfaction.

What We Offer

  • 🟠 Verified land and housing sales
  • 🟠 Property inspection and guidance
  • 🟠 Custom property marketing for landowners
  • 🟠 WhatsApp-based support and fast response
  • 🟠 Property availability across Lagos, Ogun, Oyo and more

Where We Operate

We currently provide verified listings and support in:

  • 🏙️ Lagos (Lekki, Ajah, Ikorodu, Ikeja)
  • 🏞️ Ogun (Sango Ota, Ifo, Abeokuta)
  • 🏡 Oyo (Ibadan)
  • 🌍 Expanding soon to other key cities across Nigeria

Why Choose Jubex?

  • ✔️ 100% Transparent transactions
  • ✔️ Friendly, fast customer support via WhatsApp or call
  • ✔️ Locally knowledgeable — we understand the Nigerian property market
  • ✔️ No hidden charges. No agent games. Only verified listings.
